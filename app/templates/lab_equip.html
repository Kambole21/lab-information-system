Laboratory Equipment

Explore the essential tools used in our agricultural research laboratory, each designed to enhance precision and efficiency in our experiments.

Spectrophotometer

Measures the intensity of light absorbed by a sample, crucial for analyzing soil and plant nutrient content.

Centrifuge

Separates components of a sample by spinning at high speeds, used for soil and plant tissue analysis.

PCR Machine

Amplifies DNA segments for genetic analysis of crops, aiding in disease and trait identification.

Microscope

Magnifies small samples for detailed observation of plant cells and soil microorganisms.

Autoclave

Sterilizes equipment and media using high-pressure steam, ensuring contamination-free experiments.
